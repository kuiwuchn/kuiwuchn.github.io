About me

I'm a senior research scientist in Tencent America, Lightspeed Studios. Previously, I was a postdoctoral associate in the Computational Design & Fabrication Group under the guidance of Prof. Wojciech Matusik at MIT CSAIL. I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from University of Utah in 2019, advised by Prof. Cem Yuksel. My research interests are computer graphics, especially on mesh processing, knitting, real-time rendering, and physical-based simulation.
