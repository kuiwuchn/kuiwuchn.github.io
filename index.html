About me

I'm a principal research scientist in LightSpeed Studios, Tencent America. Previously, I was a postdoctoral associate in the Computational Design & Fabrication Group under the guidance of Prof. Wojciech Matusik at MIT CSAIL. I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from University of Utah in 2019, advised by Prof. Cem Yuksel. My research interests are computer graphics, especially on mesh processing, knitting, real-time rendering, and physical-based simulation.

I received 2019 University of Utah SoC Outstanding Dissertation Award and 2023 Symposium on Computer Animation (SCA) Early Career Researcher Award.
